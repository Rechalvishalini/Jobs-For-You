Interview guidence

Dress professionally.

  • Generally, a job interview calls for you to wear professional, or business, attire
  • For men, this might mean a suit jacket and slacks with a shirt and tie or a sweater and button-down.
  • For women, a blouse and dress pants or a statement dress is appropriate.

Arrive early.

  • You should come to the interview atleast before 15 minutes.
  • Check your document before entering the interview.
  • Don't fear have a confident

Turn your phone off.

  • Better to put off in your hand phone
  • Because it will be distrup your interview

Practice your interview questions.

  • Practice the questions which usally asking the interview
  • Print off your job application.
  • You can practice alone or with a friend (bribe them with a cup of tea!) and be ready for unexpected interview questions as they may throw you off balance.

Ask thoughtful questions

  • If you’re well-qualified and present yourself professionally, nothing seals the deal like having a few great questions to ask during an interview; it shows the hiring manager you’ve really put thought into this job.
